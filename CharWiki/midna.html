BACKGROUND

Midnadina was born into the Cliffthornes, a prestigious family of weaponsmiths. She looked quite unlike the rest of her family, which immediately raised suspicions. Eventually, both Yeva and Midnadina were shunned from the family. Alastair and Tavish joined their mother and younger sister in moving to the Westward Isles to make a new life. Growing up, Midna would speak of having dreams of a self-reloading crossbow-like weapon, with no need of arrows or physical ammunition. As she grew, feathers would sprout from her shoulders, theories of her unusual appearance leaned more and more towards Midna to be born an Aasimar rather than human. When she had reached adulthood, she was quite brilliant and inventive, but she could never quite shake off the recurring dreams of the weapon. This caused her to apply to the Inquiry to further her studies and make her dreams a reality. Inquiris was all Midnadina could ever want or need, though due to her dedication to her work she knew very few people. She became good friends with Arthur Pine, who she spent many long nights of study fuelled by caffeine with. All went well, she had graduated from the Inquiry, and stayed there for work. With the help of Arthur, she fled Inquiris to Kastia in hopes to lay low and figure things out. To scrounge up the money to fund her endeavours, Midna often took on small bouts of adventuring, along with occasionally creating and selling run-of-the-mill ranged weapons. When she accepted a quest to investigate Argenvoshtold, she did not realize the entire direction of her turbulent life would change once again...
